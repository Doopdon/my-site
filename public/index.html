About This Site

This page has no frameworks, plugins or addons. It is a pure handwritten HTML/CSS/Javascript web page.

This site was made with the help of a demo by Keith Clark and art by Grant Yun, and I happily give them credit for code, color schemes, and art direction.

But this was not simple copy paste.

I used the demo by Keith, and repurposed it to work with my vision. I needed to simplify his style sheet and make it generic enough to just list sections without extra work. Originally it had hard coded z-indexes which I made unnecessary, and the sections were not responsive in a way that would allow for variable length content without changing layer alignment. It took quite a few attempts to get it working in a way I felt was maintainable.

As for the art, I took static images and turned them into responsive web elements. I kept the positioning and color schemes, but I plan on adding more and diverging from his original work more as time goes on.
